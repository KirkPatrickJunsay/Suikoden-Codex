# Privacy Policy — Suikoden Codex

**Effective date:** 25 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the **Suikoden Codex** mobile application ("the app", "we", "us"), developed by **Kirk (Codes & Chips)**, handles your information. By using the app you agree to this policy.

Suikoden Codex is a free, offline fan encyclopedia. It is designed to respect your privacy: **we do not collect, store, or share any personal information.**

## 1. Information we collect

**None.** The app does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal or sensitive user data. Specifically, the app does **not**:

- require an account, login, email, or any sign-up;
- collect your name, contacts, location, or device identifiers;
- include advertising, ad networks, or marketing trackers;
- include analytics or crash-reporting SDKs;
- send any of your information to us or to any third party.

## 2. Information stored on your device

All app data stays **only on your device** and is never uploaded. This includes:

- your recruitment progress and the entries you mark as favorites;
- recently viewed entries and app preferences (e.g. theme, spoiler-safe mode);
- any content you create within the app.

This data is stored using the device's standard local app-storage. You can remove it at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

## 3. Permissions

The app may request the standard **Internet / network-state** permission that is included by default with the development framework. The app functions fully offline and does not use the network to collect or transmit your data.

## 4. Sharing and backups

Any "share" or "backup/export" feature is **initiated only by you**. When you choose to share or export, you select the destination (for example another app or your own files) using your device's system share sheet. We do not receive a copy of anything you share or back up.

## 5. Third-party content

The app displays Suikoden game information and imagery for reference. *Suikoden* and related content are © Konami Digital Entertainment; encyclopedia text is sourced from the Gensopedia community wiki. These are shown within the app and do not involve sharing your data.

## 6. Children's privacy

The app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. It contains no ads and no data collection, and is suitable for general audiences.

## 7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
